# 🌌 GRIDRUNNER

A futuristic cyberpunk endless runner built using **Three.js**, featuring a neon-themed world, smooth animations, dynamic obstacles, responsive controls, and progressively increasing difficulty.

# 🎯 Gameplay

The objective is simple:

- Survive as long as possible.
- Avoid obstacles.
- Collect coins.
- Increase your score.
- Beat your highest score.

As the game progresses:

- Speed continuously increases.
- Obstacles become more challenging.
- Reaction time becomes critical.

---

# 🧠 Game Mechanics

### Player

- Three lane movement
- Jump mechanic
- Slide mechanic
- Collision detection
- Temporary invincibility after taking damage

---

### Obstacles

Two obstacle types are available:

#### Ground Obstacles

- Must Jump

#### Overhead Obstacles

- Must Slide

---

### Coins

Coins spawn in available lanes.

Collecting coins increases your score.

---

### Health System

Player starts with:

❤️ ❤️ ❤️

Each collision removes one life.

Game ends after losing all lives.

---

### Score System

Score increases through:

- Survival time
- Coin collection

High score is automatically stored in browser Local Storage.
